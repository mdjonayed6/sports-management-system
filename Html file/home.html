Exploring the Heart of Sports
Your Ultimate Persona

Join us at IUBAT Sports Club (ISP) and be part of a dynamic community where passion meets performance. We're dedicated to fostering a culture of excellence and sportsmanship among our students and faculty. From exciting matches to friendly games, our wide range of sports activities has something everyone can enjoy and excel in.

Prof Dr M Alimullah Miyan
(1942-2017)

Sports are not merely about physical activity or winning competitions; they encompass a myriad of values that are essential for personal and professional growth. Through sports, we learn the importance of teamwork, discipline, perseverance, and leadership - qualities that are indispensable in every aspect of life. At our university, we recognize the significance of sports in nurturing well-rounded individuals. That is why we have dedicated resources and facilities to promote a culture of athleticism and sportsmanship. Whether it's cricket, football, basketball, or any other sport, we encourage all our students to participate actively and reap the benefits that sports offer.

Our Functionality
(1942-2017)

We are proud to introduce the IUBAT Sports Club (ISP), dedicated to tracking various sports activities including Cricket, Football etc. Our platform facilitates scheduling and managing matches, providing users with access to upcoming fixtures and match results. Additionally, players, teams, and leagues can register through the website, streamlining organization and communication within our vibrant sports community. Stay updated with the latest news, achievements, and events through our dedicated sections. Join us in fostering a sense of camaraderie and engagement by participating in our interactive forums and discussion boards. Welcome to the dynamic world of sports at IUBAT University!
